We know that as an incoming freshman you may not know what you want to do as a major. If you are undecided or not sure if the major you chose is right for you, try taking some of these quizzes! These quizzes will suggest what your major should be based on your answers.

Villanova University also provides you with helpful resources on exploring majors.

Quizzes
  • ASA Futurescape
 This webstie is designed to determine your future with an interactive quiz. Through their 11 question quiz, the site predicts your personality and recommends potential futures for you. Try this quiz if you want to know what field you want to go into in the future.
  • Indigo Pathway
 This one is your basic "What's My Major" quiz. Just 10 minutes and it suggests you highly accurate and highly currated matches based on your strengths, motivators, and behaviors.
